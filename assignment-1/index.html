Working with stakeholders of the web

Different organizations from around the world join the World Wide Web Consortium as members. These members work together to help guide the development of important web technologies and share ideas with people from research and industry. A few member organization logos are shown below and they change from time to time.

The Web Consortium and its members also work closely with the public and the web community. They focus on different business areas that are influenced by the web, such as E-commerce, Media & Entertainment, Publishing and many other fields.
